NFT MINTING DAPP - Solidity, Javascript, React

This is an NFT Minting and Staking Decentralized Application that I built for My NFT Project which is named the "Crooks of Cronos" Or now Officially The "CRO County NFT Ecosystem".

This Dapp enables user's to learn about our NFTs and also Purchase or Mint an NFT which will be instantly sent to their connected wallet address. These NFTs are on the Cronos Network and can be viewed on one of the major marketplaces such as Crosea.io

Current Holders are also able to do more things on the dapp such as Stake their NFTs for Passive Crypto Rewards in the form of our own Token called CroGOLD or $cGOLD. This token is a rewards token usable within our NFT Economy for a variety of different things such as Minting exclusive NFTs.
